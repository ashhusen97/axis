"How

I

Work"

My aim is to help you find your unique ‘root’ to long-term improvements to your health. When I take you as a client, regardless of what your condition is, I take a full case history, assess all the information and provide programs to suit your needs.

MY APPROACH
I practice using the principles of Functional Medicine looking at your timeline along with your current presenting symptoms. Symptoms are not looked at individually like an isolated bodily system but the whole person is looked at.
For example, factors such as lifestyle, stress, sleep, genes and environment are all discussed during the consultation, as well as food.

THE PLAN
One of the fundamental beliefs for my practice is that there is no ‘one size fits all’ diet; likewise, there is no one health treatment which would benefit all persons at every stage of their life.
I thoroughly assess and research to find personalised solutions. To achieve long-standing outcomes, we set SMART goals for each stage of your plan and not a protocol that only treats symptoms.

Functional

Testing

In addition to your personalised nutrition plan and possible supplement suggestions, I may advise some specific functional testing. I am registered with a range of private laboratories, where we can run further tests in order to help us gain a deeper understanding of the drivers of your health condition.

Some examples of these tests include:
Food allergy / intolerance tests
Comprehensive stool analysis
Digestive function and SIBO
Genetic testing
Hormone testing: oestrogen, testosterone, cortisol
Thyroid,
Vitamin testing
